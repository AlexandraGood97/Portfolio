ABOUT ME

I'm a driven, motivated product design engineer based in Liverpool, UK. I became fascintated with technology while at secondary school, and developed a passion for using tech for good causes. I believe in using an empathetic and human-centered approach to product design, and love working on ethical engineering projects. I am currently looking to continue my career within the tech industry, in a role where I can further my interests and develop my skills.

When I'm not working, you can find me volunteering, walking my dog or tinkering on my newest side project.
